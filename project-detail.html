Living in Cítov

All amenities are available in Cítov, which is only 8 km from Mělník and 12 km from Roudnice nad Labem. Here you will find a kindergarten and primary school, a summer swimming pool, a local public library and a municipal cultural center. Sports lovers will also enjoy themselves thanks to the area with football, volleyball and universal pitches and tennis courts. In addition, from this location there is a beautiful view of the Czech landmark, Mount Říp.

In this locality, which is located in the Central Bohemian Region, 8 km from the town of Mělník and 12 km from Roudnice nad Labem, we are preparing the construction of two semi-detached houses with their own land and parking space.

At the moment we are waiting for the approval of the subdivision of the land. We are finalizing the project documentation for obtaining a building permit for the houses and selecting a contractor to build the driveway.
